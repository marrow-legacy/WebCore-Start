This web application will help step you through the process of creating a web application of your own. Along the top of the pages presented here is a menu of options. It's suggested that you progress through these options in order as later settings may depend on earlier ones. Feel free to go back and add additional detail if you need to, though.

If at any point you require assistance feel free to drop by the support chat room. You can find us on irc.freenode.net in the #webcore channel. We're here to help.
